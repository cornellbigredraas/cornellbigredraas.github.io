About

Founded in 2005, Cornell Big Red Raas is a nationally ranked garba-raas teams. Garba-raas is a traditional dance form from Gujarat, India. Utilizing their passion and energy, Big Red Raas revamps this folk dance with modern moves to create truly jaw-dropping performances. Having won several competitions and awards in cities around the nation and on campus, Big Red Raas has developed a reputation of spreading joy and awe everywhere they go. One team, one love, Big Red Raas.
