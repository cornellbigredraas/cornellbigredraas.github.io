Who Are We?

Aurora Consulting is a student-run organization sponsored by University of California, Berkeley. Aurora focuses on providing consulting services backed by articulate numerical analysis and quantitative modeling to established companies. Our primary goal is to provide insightful, strategic, and unique solutions for problems that our clients are facing.

What Do We Do?

Through keen financial decision making and quantitative reasoning, our members develop technically sound solutions for challenges our clients are facing. We utilize many engineering skills and practices such as financial engineering, data science, optimization techniques, etc. to offer supported solutions for our clients. Each client engagement is about a semester long, in which teams work together to provide meaningful deliverables.

Why Should I Join?

As a newly established organization, we welcome all types of students who have an interest in business, finance, engineering, quantitative analysis, and most importantly, a desire to learn. We provide a comfortable, open environment where all of our members will closely work together, engage in thoughtful discussions, and form valuable relationships.

Come join to heighten your undergraduate experience with new skills, networking opportunities, and lifelong friendships!
