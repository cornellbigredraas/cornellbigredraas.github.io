About

Founded in 2005, Cornell Big Red Raas is a nationally ranked garba-raas teams. Garba-raas is a traditional dance form from Gujarat, India. Utilizing their passion and energy, Big Red Raas revamps this folk dance with modern moves to create truly jaw-dropping performances. Having won several competitions and awards in cities around the nation and on campus, Big Red Raas has developed a reputation of spreading joy and awe everywhere they go. One team, one love, Big Red Raas.

Tryouts

Tryouts for Fall 2020 will be held virtually! We're holding the following events:

  • Meet the Team: Zoom Edition | Sunday, 9/13 @8pm EST
    Get to know some of our members, get information about our team, and ask us questions about the team or Cornell in general! This event is optional, but we would love to meet everyone!
    Zoom link
  • Tryout Choreo Released | Sunday, 9/20
    We will release a video of the tryout choreo to Instagram, Facebook, and Youtube.
  • Tryout Days | Monday, 9/21 @7pm EST and Saturday, 9/26 @2pm EST
    Tryout days will be hosted through a live zoom call where we will teach the tryout choreo step-by-step and give you a chance to ask our dancers any questions you have. This is the easiest way for you to learn the choreo, so we recommend coming to one of the days! At the end, you can do the choreo for us in breakout room as your tryout or you can submit your own video anytime before the deadline. (The two days will be the same, so you do not have to attend both.)
  • Tryout Videos Due | Sunday, 9/20 @ 11:59pm EST
    If you did not have us record you during a tryout day, submit a video of you doing the tryout choreo by this day. The form for responses will be available once tryouts begin.

We're so excited to meet you all, and if you have any questions, feel free to DM us on Instagram or email us!
